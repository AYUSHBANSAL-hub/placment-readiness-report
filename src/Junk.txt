Step 1: Isa mail is sent to the candidate requesting submission within 7 days.
Step 2: After verifying the ISA documents we keept it. A dedicated Batch Manager schedules a call with the student to welcome him/her and let them know about the details of the classes and the general functioning.
Step 3: Students are then added to the Whatsapp groups so they can communicate with each other as well as the batch manager
Step 4: A 1 on 1 video call with the Batch Manager to clear all the confusion.
Step 5: Portal is then activated 
Step 6: Classes start
Step 7: Regular followups by Batch Manager
